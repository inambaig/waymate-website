Built to be used for free

Waymate is designed so everyday commuting stays affordable. Join for free, complete simple milestones, and you're set for your first 10 trips — no credit card, no subscription, no catch.

To keep things free for everyone, you'll occasionally see small ads while browsing the app — never when you're booking a ride, accepting a trip, chatting, or making a payment. Want more WayPoints? Earn them through rewards, invite friends, or watch optional videos. It's your choice.
