1. Introduction

Waymate ("Waymate," "we," "us," or "our") operates a subscription-based carpooling platform that connects verified Ride Hosts and Passengers for recurring shared commutes in Pakistan. Our mobile applications (iOS and Android), website at waymate.pk, administrative systems, and all associated services are collectively referred to as the "Services."

This Privacy Policy explains, in detail, what personal information we collect, why we collect it, how we use and share it, how long we keep it, and what rights you may have under applicable law. This Policy applies to all individuals who interact with Waymate, including Ride Hosts, Passengers, website visitors, support correspondents, referral participants, and individuals whose data is submitted to us by another user (for example, emergency contacts or individuals named in a live-tracking share link).

This Privacy Policy must be read together with our Terms of Use, which govern your use of the Services and contain extensive limitations of liability, indemnification, and release provisions.     • Mobile phone number (required for account creation and authentication);

    • Usage role (Passenger, Host, or both);
    • Email address (if provided);

    • Referral code and referral relationship data;
    • Verification badges (verified Passenger, verified Host);
    • Account status (active, deactivated, deleted);
    • Ratings and review aggregates (host rating, passenger rating, and counts).

    5.2 Identity Verification Documents

    For Hosts and Passengers seeking verified status, we collect and store:

    • Computerized National Identity Card (CNIC) — front and back images;
    • Live or uploaded selfie for identity matching;
    • Driving license (required for Host verification);
    • Character certificate (where applicable);
    • Document verification status, review timestamps, and administrative notes.

    5.3 Vehicle Information (Hosts)

    • Vehicle type, brand, model, variant, year, and color;

    • Vehicle features and safety attributes;
    • Vehicle photographs (front and back);
    • Owner notes and default vehicle designation.

    5.4 Trip, Route & Booking Data

    • Trip names, departure times, recurrence patterns, and operating days;
    • Start and end location labels, coordinates, route polylines, and distance/duration estimates;
    • Price ranges, seat availability, gender preferences, and comfort features;
    • Trip requests, booking confirmations, seat positions, agreed fares, and cancellation records;
    • Booking series for recurring commutes;
    • Trip run status, start/completion timestamps, and session records;
    • Pickup and drop-off coordinates for passenger sessions;
    • Search snapshots captured at the time of booking or request.

    5.5 Location & GPS Data

    • Real-time GPS coordinates during active Host trip runs (latitude, longitude, speed, heading, accuracy, and timestamp);
    • Append-only location chunks forming a full GPS trail for completed trips;
    • Last known location of active trips;
    • Traveled route polylines after trip completion;
    • Proximity event data (for example, when a Host is within a defined distance of a Passenger pickup point);
    • Device location permissions status and related technical metadata.

    5.6 Communications Data

    • In-app chat messages (text and images) between Hosts and Passengers;
    • Message delivery and read receipts;
    • Conversation metadata (titles, status, timestamps, unread counts);
    • WhatsApp OTP codes and delivery status (we store hashed OTP values, not plaintext codes);
    • WhatsApp webhook events and message metadata where applicable;
    • Push notification tokens (Expo push token and platform identifier);
    • In-app notification history.

    5.7 Safety, Reviews & Support Data

    • Trip reviews (scores, tags, and comments) submitted by Hosts and Passengers;
    • Safety flags (including categories such as harassment, dangerous driving, no-show, smoking, misbehavior, fake profile, and vehicle mismatch);
    • Support tickets (subject, message, category, emergency flag, and associated trip references);
    • AI support chat conversations, messages, and tool-call logs;
    • Profile field change history (name, email, city edits).

    5.8 Payment, Subscription & Monetization Data

    • Subscription plan, price, trial price, start/end dates, and status;
    • WayPoints ledger transactions (earnings, redemptions, booking fees, referral rewards, ad rewards, admin adjustments);
    • Waypoint purchase records and package selections;
    • Payment processor transaction references (we do not store full payment card numbers on our servers);
    • Ad interaction data where rewarded or banner advertisements are displayed.

    5.9 Live Tracking Share Data

    • Share tokens, creation timestamps, expiry and revocation records;
    • Track anchor coordinates and timestamps for shared views;
    • Identity of the user who created the share link;
    • Access logs related to shared tracking URLs where technically feasible.

    5.10 Technical & Device Information

    • IP address, network information, and general device identifiers;
    • Crash logs, diagnostic data, and performance metrics;
    • Authentication session data and security event logs;

    9. Location Data & Live Tracking

    Location data is essential to Waymate's carpooling functionality. When a Host starts a trip run, the app may collect continuous or periodic GPS samples that are transmitted to our servers and associated with the active trip session.

    9.1 What Location Data Is Collected

    During active trip runs, we may collect latitude, longitude, speed, heading, accuracy estimates, and timestamps. After trips complete, we may store a merged polyline representing the traveled route.

    9.2 Who Can See Location Data

    • Passengers with confirmed bookings on the active trip may view live Host location during the trip run through the app;
    • Users who create tracking share links may share live tracking with third parties via tokenized URLs, subject to expiry and revocation controls;
    • Waymate personnel may access location data for safety investigations, support, fraud prevention, and legal compliance;
    • Location data is not made publicly searchable or published on open directories.

    9.3 Your Controls

    You may revoke location permissions at the device level; however, certain features (including hosting trips with live tracking) will not function without location access during active trip runs. You may revoke tracking share links before their expiry where the app provides revocation controls.

    9.4 No Liability for Location Data

You use location features and share tracking links at your own risk. Waymate is not liable for unauthorized access to location data caused by other users, share-link recipients, device compromise, or user error, except where liability cannot be excluded under mandatory applicable law.
