About Waymate

Waymate is on a mission to make carpooling in Pakistan friendly, social, and stress-free — turning empty seats into shared journeys and strangers into mates along the way.

Inam ur Rehman B.

Founding CEO of Waymate

  • Founding Director of The Glance Systems open_in_new
  • Founding CTO of Slymbee open_in_new

Inam has spent over a decade building commuter-first products — from enterprise platforms to consumer apps — always with a focus on real people and everyday journeys. Waymate is the culmination of that experience: a product built for Pakistan's commuters, by someone who understands the daily grind of getting from A to B.
