By using Waymate, you agree to these terms. Waymate is a platform connecting Ride Hosts and Passengers for shared commutes. We do not operate vehicles or employ drivers.

Users must complete identity verification to access full features. Hosts must provide valid CNIC, selfie, and driving license documentation.

Subscription fees are billed monthly. Fair usage limits apply as described in the app.

Privacy Policy

Waymate collects information necessary to provide carpooling services, including phone number, profile details, location data for routes and live tracking, and verification documents.

Location data is used for route matching, trip tracking, and safety features. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at the email address listed on this site.
